# Zhang Yingfa

Technical Support & Equipment Assurance Specialist
 
Focusing on the hardware implementation and equipment provision for the three major projects, Zhang Yingfa undertakes core technical support work. For the rocket launch project, he precisely completes the 3D modeling and printing of rocket body structures and propulsion system components, ensuring dimensional accuracy and assembly compatibility; he provides key experimental equipment such as sensors, controllers, and display terminals for the smart campus system construction process, and assists in hardware debugging and adaptation; for the optical fiber computer conceptual design, he creates visualization models of core components to intuitively present the conceptual architecture. Additionally, he is responsible for quality inspection of all printed parts and maintenance management of experimental equipment, ensuring the stability and reliability of hardware aspects for all projects.

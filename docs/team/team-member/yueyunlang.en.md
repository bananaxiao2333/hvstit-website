# Yue Yunlang

Assembly Process & Chemical Application Researcher
 
Focusing on the physical manifestation of projects and specialized technical research, providing core support in the fields of assembly and chemical applications. In the rocket launch project, he is responsible for the precise assembly and sealing treatment of various rocket body components, completing the painting and coloring of the rocket body to enhance aerodynamic performance and visual presentation; during the smart campus system construction phase, he participates in equipment integration and assembly, and researches the application of chemical sensing materials in environmental monitoring modules to optimize system data collection accuracy; for the optical fiber computer conceptual design, he deeply explores the characteristics of chemical coating materials related to optical signal transmission, providing chemical technical references for concept implementation, while participating in the detailed assembly and effect optimization of models, ensuring the scientific validity and completeness of concept visualization.
